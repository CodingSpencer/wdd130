Merch

Nothing says you're a Captain America fan like having your own merch. Whether it's a shirt, poster, or a metal painted shield. Merch varies on price so one would want to ensure you find things that fit your price range. If you want a more expensive item make a plan to save up some funds to purchase it. Below are listed some of the best merch options from different websites.

Captain America Endgame Funko

This is a figure of Captain America in Avengers Endgame, the highest grossing film of all time.

Captain America Metal Shield

This shield is seen in many of the comics and in almost all the MCU films including Chris Evans.

Captain America T-Shirt

This shirt has an amazing collage of all multiple scenes of Chris Evans in the MCU.

Captain America Bath Rug

If you don't have enough Captain America stuff, you might as well get a rug for your bathroom.

Captain America Lego Figure

If you've ever owned any Legos you want a Lego Captain America.

2-Figure Captain America Pack

This pack comes with a MCU duo. Includes two Captain America's, Sam Wilson and Steve Rogers.
